About Me
I am a musician and actor based in Gothenburg, Sweden.
I've just had my first bit of experience with acting, playing several parts in an amateur rendition of Hair in Lidköping.

In the fall of 2026 I will be joining Teater Scarlet in Gothenburg for a murder mystery play set in the 1700's, centering around the murder of King Gustav III of Sweden.

My current day job is massage, and on my free time I'm teaching myself web and game development (I made this website myself from scratch!), writing music, singing in a choir and fiddling with various other creative projects.

I also love yoga, running and resistance training and try to incorporate this into my everyday life to keep my body and mind running smoothly.
