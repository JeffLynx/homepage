About Me

Musician and actor based in Gothenburg, Sweden. My most recent acting experience was playing several parts in an amateur rendition of Hair in Lidköping. This year I will be joining Teater Scarlet in Gothenburg for a murder mystery play set in the 1700's, centering around the murder of King Gustav III of Sweden. My current day job is massage, and on my free time I'm teaching myself web and game development (I made this website myself from scratch!), and dabbling in various creative pursuits like writing music and singing in a choir.
